i About Us

Imototo Cleaning Services is a Bolton based cleaning company providing professional cleaning services for letting agents, landlords, property managers, Airbnb hosts and homeowners across Greater Manchester and surrounding areas.

We understand the importance of preparing properties quickly and to a high standard. Our team delivers reliable, thorough cleaning that helps ensure properties are presented in the best possible condition for new tenants, viewings, inspections and guest arrivals.

Fully insured and committed to excellence, we pride ourselves on professionalism, attention to detail and dependable service.
